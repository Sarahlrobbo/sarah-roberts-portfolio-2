Behind the screens

👋 Hi, I'm Sarah, a design leader, researcher, systems thinker and problem-solver.

I've spent over a decade designing digital products, services, and systems across startups, SaaS, the public sector, and high-impact industries. I care deeply about products that make life easier for others — whether that's simplifying essential tasks, improving clarity, or removing cognitive load when it matters most.
