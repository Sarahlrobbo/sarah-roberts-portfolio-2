Philosophy

How I lead & design

The principles I keep coming back to, again and again.

🎯

Clarity over complexity

The best design reduces cognitive load. For users and for teams. I make the problem, the principles, and the decision criteria unambiguous before anything else.

🧭

Evidence, not opinions

I use Jobs-To-Be-Done and Outcome-Driven Innovation to move from idea-led planning to opportunity-led discovery. Know what to build, why, and in what order.

🤖

AI as a multiplier

AI doesn't replace designers who think strategically — it sorts them. I model AI-first workflows openly and coach teams through the shift with honesty, not hype.

🧱

Systems over one-offs

Great outcomes should be repeatable. Charters, rituals, design systems, and contribution models mean teams can move fast without losing guardrails.

🌱

Grow people

I build psychological safety and shared expectations so teams thrive together. I share my own uncertainty and learning curve, rather than presenting false confidence.

🚢

Ship, then learn

I believe in releasing real value, measuring it honestly, and iterating. De-risk with speed, not more planning — then use customer feedback to shape what's next.
